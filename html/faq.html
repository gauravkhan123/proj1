Emu Oil and Antioxidant Oil Storage

Our four ounce bottles can refill our one ounce or half ounce plastic dropper bottles, glass dropper bottles and rollons that are lightweight, convenient size, perfect for dispensing oils accurately; one drop at a time, allowing your oil to last up to twice as long. These smaller bottles are a great addition to your purse, pocketbook, pocket or gym bag that allows you to store the larger four ounce bottles in your refrigerater.

How long does emu oil and other antioxidant skincare oils keep? Pure emu oil fully refined and antioxidant oils normally last up to 2 years at room temperature, up to twice as long when refrigerated. Years in the freezer. Your Emu Oil will become solid when refrigerated or frozen.

Do not freeze any product contained in glass.

Keep your emu oil that you use daily at under 75 degree fahrenheit or 24 degrees celsius at home or in the office, out of direct sunlight. When traveling keep Emu Oil in a pocket, gym bag, purse but do not leave antioxidant skincare in the car or anywhere hot.

Keep what you do not use on daily basis refrigerated. Defrost at room temperature and shake well before using.
