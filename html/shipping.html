Shipping Policies

Nutricare Plus has some answers that can work for you!

We start the shipping process the day we receive your order.

We ship USPS First Class Mail, delivery in 1 to 5 days or USPS Priority Mail, deliver 2 to 3 days in the USA. We also offer a flat shipping rate of $4.99 for orders under $49.00 for the USA, FPO, Hawaii, Alaska, and U.S. Territories using USPS First Class Mail with tracking for orders under fifteen ounces and USPS Priority Mail for orders over fifteen ounces; giving you the exact cost before checkout so there are no surprises. If your order is over $49.00 there will be no shipping cost except international orders and wholesale orders.

NOTE: Presently our shop does not process international orders online.

Please email us with your order and we will send you a PayPal invoice to pay for your order.

Checkout with PayPal

As soon as you submit your order by clicking on the “Checkout with PayPal” button; you will be directed to PayPal’s secure payment site. Credit card or eCheck details are collected securely using PayPal.

If you do not have a PayPal account. Payment can be made securely with your credit card or eCheck without logging on to PayPal.

International Shipping

We use DHL Express Mail or USPS mail for International Service with a flat rate cost of appromiately $16.00 USD for USPS and $38.00 for DHL for international shipments.

If you wish to use USPS, or DHL International Service; shipping costs will be confirmed by email. We have had great success using both International Services. We will email you a pdf image of the confirmation notice with a copy of the International Shipping Label when we mail your package.

We can be reach using Skype: nutricareplus EST 4AM – 11 for a friendly voice to complete your order or contact us by clicking on this link so you can email us regarding shipping costs which must be verified.

If you do not want to use PayPal when ordering, just call us for a friendly voice to complete your order or email us if you have questions about your order.

Every order placed with us receives the Nutricare Plus attention to detail, along with the service and delivery you expect.

Florida Residents: 6 percent sales tax will be added to your total.
